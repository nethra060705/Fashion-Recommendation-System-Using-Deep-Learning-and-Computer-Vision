## üîß **HOW DOES IT WORK? (THE SIMPLE VERSION)**

### **Step 1: Smart Looking**
When you upload a photo of clothes:
- Our AI automatically finds the clothing item in the photo
- It ignores backgrounds, people, and other distracting stuff
- It's like having smart eyes that focus only on the clothes

### **Step 2: Understanding Style**
The AI analyzes the clothing:
- **Color**: Not just "red" but what kind of red, where it appears, how much
- **Pattern**: Stripes, flowers, solid, checkered, etc.
- **Texture**: Smooth, rough, shiny, matte
- **Style**: Casual, formal, vintage, modern
- **Shape**: How it fits, the silhouette, the cut

### **Step 3: Creating a Fashion Fingerprint**
- The AI converts everything it sees into a unique "fingerprint"
- This fingerprint is made of 512 numbers that capture the essence of the clothing
- Like how every person has unique fingerprints, every piece of clothing gets unique numbers

### **Step 4: Finding Matches**
- The AI compares your item's fingerprint with thousands of others
- It finds the most similar items super quickly
- But it's smart enough to include variety - not just identical items

### **Step 5: Smart Suggestions**
- Instead of showing 5 identical red shirts, it might show:
  - 2 similar red tops (because that's what you want)
  - 1 pink blouse (similar color family)
  - 1 red dress (same color, different style)
  - 1 white shirt (different color, same style)
